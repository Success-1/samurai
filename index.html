About Us

We are a community-driven organization committed to combating child malnutrition. With a focus on early intervention, nutrition education, and family-centered care, our goal is to reduce stunting and underweight rates among children under five. Together with local partners and health workers, we're building a healthier future, one child at a time.

Problem Statement

Malnultrition remains a significant health issue in the nyamesheke district, located in rwanda's western province. Despite various interventions, the district continues to experience high rates of stunting and underweight in children under the age of five..

Mission Statement

By December 2027, we will reduce child stunting in the Nyamasheke community by at least 10 percentage points (e.g. from 33% to 23%), by training 200 caregivers in nutrition education annually, supporting 500 households to establish home gardens, and providing access to micronutrient-rich food resources; all in partnership with local health centres and community actors.

Our Solution

We believe that ending child malnutrition begins at the heart of the community. Our approach combines nutrition support, health education, and early childhood interventions tailored to the specific needs of families in Nyamasheke District. By empowering caregivers, training local health workers, and delivering essential resources, we're not just addressing the symptoms of malnutrition we're breaking the cycle and building lasting change for generations to come.
